WHY THE BLOCKCHAIN?

Blockchain technology has been widely accepted in recent years and will surely be widespread in the near future. Blockchain is the right ally for a forward-looking project like AMG. It permits information to be stored and transferred (in this case, transactions) without passing via a central intermediate institution. Above all, the blockchain makes it possible to link individuals, making it feasible for anybody worldwide the world to engage in a project like AMG. Indeed, it would seem difficult for an American to invest in real estate in Europe without going through all the administrative and banking obstacles that blockchain can address.
